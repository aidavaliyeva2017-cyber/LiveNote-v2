# LiveNote 

> AI-powered calendar that turns plain language into a structured schedule — built for students.

## What is LiveNote?

LiveNote eliminates the friction of traditional planning. Instead of manually managing a calendar, you simply type what you need — and LiveNote handles the rest. It builds your baseline schedule from recurring commitments, places events from natural language input, and automatically generates exam study plans around your real availability.

**Core promise:** Type it once → it appears in your calendar, with reminders and plans.

---

## Features

- **Natural Language Input** — Type "Doctor Friday 16:00" and it's instantly added to your calendar with a suggested reminder.
- **Baseline Schedule Builder** — Set up your weekly recurring commitments and routines in minutes during onboarding.
- **AI Exam Planner** — Type "Biology exam March 10. Make me a plan." and LiveNote schedules focused study sessions into your free time — automatically.
- **Smart Conflict Detection** — Warns you of overlaps and proposes the next available slot.
- **Today View** — An AI-generated daily summary so you always know what's coming up.
- **Reminders** — Default and per-event reminders delivered at the right local time.
- **Calendar Views** — Day, Week, and Month views, always in sync.

---

## Who is it for?

LiveNote V1 is built for **students** — high school and university — who have recurring schedules and upcoming exams. If you're overwhelmed by planning or just keep forgetting things, LiveNote is for you.
